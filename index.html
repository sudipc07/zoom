About

About Zoom 16

Zoom 16 began in Kolkata in 1980, at a time when colour photo printing was still a rare and evolving craft.

From the beginning, the goal was simple: to help families preserve their most meaningful memories with care, quality, and lasting beauty.

Over the decades, the studio has grown alongside the city it serves. Generations of families have trusted Zoom 16 with their wedding albums, family photographs, and special moments.

While printing technology has evolved dramatically, the commitment to craftsmanship and personal attention remains unchanged.

Today, Zoom 16 continues that tradition, combining modern printing capabilities with experience built over more than forty years.
